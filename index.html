  • Welcome to Diva Dogs veterinary Hospital


    DIVA DOGS is a grooming salon that offers a one-on-one service to cater to each dog's individual needs in an unhurried and caring atmosphere. They believe that all dogs should be treated with gentleness, respect, understanding, and love. DIVA DOGS detects areas of concern during grooming and alerts owners so they can pursue veterinary advice. They use only natural and plant-based products and promote a holistic approach to animal care. DIVA DOGS aims to provide superior quality clipping and grooming of all breeds and transform nervous or sensitive dogs into relaxed and happy customers.
